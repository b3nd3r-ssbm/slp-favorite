Rename Favorited Files




Rename Syntax:
Separate all values by a comma (,)

Values will be separated by a dash (-) in the names, although if time is selected the colons (:) will be replaced with underscores (_)

Possible Values

p1N (player 1 name)

p2N (player 2 name)

p1T (player 1 tag)

p2T (player 2 tag)

dd (day)

mm (month)

yyyy (year)

p1LC (player 1 long character name)

p2LC (player 2 long character name)

p1SC (player 1 short character name)

p2SC (player 2 short character name)

time (time of the game, in your local time zone)

LS (long stage name)

SS (short stage name)
